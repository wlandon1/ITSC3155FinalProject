What is Wellness Together?

Wellness together is a resource created by students for students who suffer from mental struggles such as depression and anxiety. For this project, we had a vision to create something which would be able to benefit others. Our goal in creating this website was to create a resource that would be able to show people that they are not alone in whatever they struggle with. We believe that by making information about mental issues available to people, we can help to reduce the amount of isolation that comes with these issues.

Who are we?

Pao Xiong

Born in Thailand in a refugee camp, mental illness was nothing too far from home. Many people who had mental illness were seen as crazy, haunted, or insane, and they never really got it treated medically. My family was similar in that aspect, as people would never really address mental illness and even at times the mental abuse was not addressed. Aside from personal relations to seeing the issue with the stigma around mental illness, the passion for this project came from wanting to help make resources, no matter which, more accessible to everyone around the world. I currently reside as a Sophomore undergraduate student at UNC Charlotte and plan to graduate Fall 2023.
